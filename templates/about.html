📚 About BookShelf

BookShelf is a personal book catalog where you can discover, track, and rate books.

🎯 Purpose

This site helps book lovers organize their reading lists, explore new titles, and share ratings with the community.

✨ Features
  • Browse books by genre, author, and year
  • View detailed info and ratings for each book
  • Rate books on a 1–5 star scale
  • Admin users can add, edit, and delete books
  • Register and log in to participate
🛠️ Built With

Flask · SQLAlchemy · Bootstrap 5 · Flask-Login · Flask-WTF
